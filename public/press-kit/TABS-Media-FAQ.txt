What is TABS?
TABS (Technology Adoption Barriers Survey) is a research initiative focused on understanding the real-world barriers organizations face when adopting new technologies. The goal is actionable insight that helps leaders prioritize interventions and improve the likelihood that technology investments deliver value.

Who is this for?
TABS is designed for organizational leaders and practitioners involved in selecting, implementing, or operating technology.

What does TABS publish?
TABS plans to publish aggregated insights and research updates. Individual responses are not published on this site.
